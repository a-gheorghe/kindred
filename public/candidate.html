FAQs
How does pricing work?
Applying for Kindred is free to candidates.
If you are placed into a company via our
platform, you owe a 10% placement fee.
This fee is based on your starting salary.
Why do you charge candidates
and not companies?
Junior candidates struggle the most in
landing job #1. Companies hire junior when
they can’t find senior candidates. Kindred is
creating an system that incentivizes
recruiters and employees to take a close
look at candidates when it’s needed the
most.
What are my chances?
Once you are on the Kindred platform you
are not guaranteed a job. However, if we
take you on, we’re fully incentivized to make
your job search as easy as possible.
How does Kindred
help with placement
We have both an internal placement
as well as a network of employeers who are
actively looking to match candidates with
their companies!
What companies are in
the KindredNetwork?
We work with companies ranging from early
stage startups to tech giants like Google
and Facebook. We match candidates based
on our belief in their likelihood in getting an
interview, with a Kindred push.
Do I need to be qualified?
Due to the size of our team, we can only
work with a certain number of candidates at
a time. We will notify you within 72 hours of
signing up our team is actively working on
placing you.
